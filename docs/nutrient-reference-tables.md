## Pregnancy Changes Summary

### Nutrients with MAJOR Increases (>40%)
1. **Iron**: +50-80% (blood volume, fetal stores)
2. **Protein**: +54% (tissue building)
3. **Folate**: +50% (cell division, neural tube)
4. **Iodine**: +47% (thyroid, brain development)
5. **Vitamin B6**: +46-58%
6. **Thiamin**: +27-40%
7. **Riboflavin**: +27-40%
8. **Carbohydrate minimum**: +35%
9. **Zinc**: +33-38%

### Nutrients with Moderate Increases (10-40%)
1. **Niacin**: +29%
2. **Omega-3 ALA**: +27%
3. **Pantothenic acid**: +20%
4. **Vitamin C**: +13-23%
5. **Fiber**: +8-33%
6. **Water**: +11-30%
7. **Magnesium**: +11-13%
8. **Potassium**: +11-13%

### Nutrients UNCHANGED
1. **Calcium** (must be consistently met)
2. **Vitamin D** (deficiency still common)
3. **Vitamin E**
4. **Vitamin K**
5. **Phosphorus**

### New Targets in Pregnancy
1. **DHA**: 200-300 mg/day (no pre-pregnancy target)
2. **Energy increments**: T2 +340, T3 +452 kcal
